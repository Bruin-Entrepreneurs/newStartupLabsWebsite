UCLA's student run accelerator program
"A program fostering students' startup ideas. We work with students and mentors to create an environment that encourages innovation and provides guidance for students and their endeavors. Startup Labs is dedicated to developing the entrepreneurial spirit at UCLA with our resources."
Our Program
  • Our yearlong program consists of networking events, visits from guest speakers, and informative sessions for business development
  • Students will be provided with resources to aid them in finance, market analysis, and other entrepreneurial topics
  • The program culminates in a Demo Day in the spring, where students present their startups to a network of experienced entrepreneurs and venture capitalists from the LA area
20/20 Dinner:
Dinner with twenty entrepreneurs and twenty students, working to develop the entrepreneurial community at UCLA
Program Kickoff:
Networking dinner between the cohort company founders and program mentors
  • Each team will be paired with an experienced mentor from the LA startup scene
  • After this, students will be encouraged to connect regularly with their mentors during office hours
  • The mentors will be available to provide guidance, advice and essential skills whilst also being a opportunity for the students to network and further their entrepreneurial careers
Featured Companies
Playfull (2016 Cohort)
Connecting video game players with real life awards. Starting out of UCLA, Playful went through Startup Labs, Startup UCLA, and eventually made it into the Techstars Accelerator program. They are currently service League of Legends players, and they are looking to grow their platform!
SikeAI (2018 Cohort)
Utilizing cutting edge AI and psychology research to bridge the gaps between individuals on remote teams. The SikeAI team has seen many successes throughout their development, having also gone through the Startup UCLA and Anderson Accelerator programs.
Duffl (2019 Cohort)
Developing a hyperlocalized platform on which students can purchase snacks and goods. Duffl immediately became a fan favorite at UCLA upon their inception, and they are continuing to develop and scale their product. The Duffl team is currently going through the Y Combinator accelerator program!
Charipay (2019 Cohort)
Building a more conducive platform for charitable actions. Charipay is working to create a tailored experience for individuals based on their charitable interests and passions. They help serve those who want to give, and want to optimize thier givings for current world issues!
